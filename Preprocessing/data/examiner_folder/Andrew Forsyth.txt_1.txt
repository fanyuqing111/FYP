Prof Andrew Forsyth
Professor
Biography
Andrew Forsyth graduated with the BSc(Eng) degree in Electrical Engineering from Imperial 
College, London, in 1981 and he obtained the PhD degree in Power Electronics from the 
University of Cambridge in 1987. Following industrial experience with GEC Electrical 
Projects, he has held lecturing positions at the universities of Bath and Birmingham and was 
appointed to the Chair in Power Electronics at Manchester in 2004 - a post that is co-
sponsored by the Royal Academy of Engineering and Rolls-Royce.
