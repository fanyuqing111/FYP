Research Interests:
Information technology and mathematical optimization for problems of strategic importance to the society, including Smart Power Systems – smart grid, auction 
methods for electricity markets, robust renewable (wind and solar) integration to the grid, electricity load and price forecasting, and micro grid; Smart and Green 
Buildings and Eco Communities – optimized energy management for buildings and chiller plants, HVAC fault detection and diagnosis, emergency crowd guidance, 
and eco communities; Intelligent Manufacturing Systems – planning, scheduling, and coordination of design, manufacturing, and service activities; and 
mathematical optimization of large-scale mixed-integer problems, and decision-making under uncertain, distributed, or antagonistic environments.
