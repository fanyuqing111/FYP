Research
Research Interests:
Antennas: UWB antennas and arrays, multiband CP antennas, RFID antennas and MIMO 
antennas
Devices: THz magnetrons and gyrotrons and quasi-optic systems
Communications: UWB communications, UWB powerline communications and MIMO systems
Imaging: UWB imaging system and THz imaging system
Bio-electromagnetics:  RF interacton with human body, Implanted antennas and UWB pulse 
electroporation of cells
