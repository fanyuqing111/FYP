Ravi Agarwal
Ravi P. Agarwal (born July 10, 1947) is an Indian 
mathematician, 
Ph.D. 
sciences, 
professor, 
Professor 
& 
Chairman, Department of Mathematics Texas A&M University-
Kingsville, Kingsville, U.S.A. Agarwal is the author of over 1000 
scientific papers as well as 30 monographs.[1]
