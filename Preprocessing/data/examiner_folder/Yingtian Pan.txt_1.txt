(2) Research Interests 
Current research of Dr. Pan’s lab is focused on early-stage epithelial cancer 
detection, diagnosis of cartilage injury and repair, and assessment of engineering 
tissue growth. In addition, Dr. Pan’s lab studies skin dehydration, geriatric 
incontinence, fluorescence imaging and light microscopy.
