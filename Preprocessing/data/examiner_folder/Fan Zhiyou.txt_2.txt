Research Interests
Fabrication and characterization of nanomaterials and nanostructures; applications of functional nanomaterials for 
electronics, energy harvesting and sensing.
Biography
Dr. Zhiyong Fan recieved his B. S. and M. S. degree in physical electronics from Fudan University, Shanghai, China, in 1998 
and 2001. He recieved Ph.D degree from University of California, Irvine in 2006 in Materials Science. From 2007 to 2010, he 
worked in University of California, Berkeley as a postdoctoral fellow in department of Electrical Engineering and Computer 
Sciences, with a joint appointment with Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory. In May 2010, he joined Hong Kong 
University of Science and Technology as an assistant professor. Dr. Fan has published more than 40 referred papers with 
more than 1000 citations. He holds 2 China and 1 US patents.
