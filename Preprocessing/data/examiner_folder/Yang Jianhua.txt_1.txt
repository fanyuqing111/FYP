Nanoelectronics and Nanoionics for computing and energy applications.
Current research thrusts are unconventional computing technologies:
1. High performance Non-volatile random access memories;
2. Analog computing using resistance analog switches;
3. Neuromorphic / Synaptic computing using memristive devices.
