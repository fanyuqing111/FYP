C.  RESEARCH  INTERESTS  &  PUBLICATIONS    
•  
My  research  activities  are  focused  on  (1)  EM  modelling  and  design  of  RF  components  and  
modules,  (2)  on  nano-­modelling  and  nanocharacterization  techniques  for  assisting  the

components  such  as  nano-­resonators,  nano  antennas,  etc.)  used  in  high-­frequency
