Biography
1998-2002: Study of Computer Science and Engineering at Hamburg University of Technology
2002-2005: DPhil Studies in Numerical Analysis at the University of Oxford
2006: Award of DPhil
2005-2006: Postdoc Position at Technical University Braunschweig
2006-2009: PDRA at the University of Manchester
2009-2011: First PDRA then EPSRC Career Acceleration Fellow at University of Reading
2011-2013: Lecturer and EPSRC Career Acceleration Fellow at UCL
2013-2018: Reader in Mathematics at UCL
since 2018: Professor of Computational Mathematics at UCL
Research Summary
My main research areas are Numerical Analysis and Scientific Computing. This branch of mathematics deals with the design, analysis and
efficient implementation of algorithms for the numerical simulation of complicated models in the Natural Sciences, which are typically
described by partial differential equations.
