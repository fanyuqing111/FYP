Brief Introduction：  
       Dr. Yu Yajun’s main research areas are (1) Energy-efficient VLSI digital signal processing 
(DSP) system and (2) DSP for communications and Biomedical Engineering. She has a publication 
record with more than 30 internal SCI indexed journal papers, and more than 30 international 
conference papers.  She is a committee member of IEEE Circuits & Systems Society, Digital Signal 
Processing Technical Committee.
