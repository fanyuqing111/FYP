Biography
Pierluigi Siano(M’09) received the M.Sc. degree in electronic engineering and the Ph.D. degree in 
information and electrical engineering from the University of Salerno, Salerno, Italy, in 2001 and 2006, 
respectively. Currently, he is an Aggregate Professor with the Department of Industrial Engineering, 
University of Salerno. His research activities are centered on the integration of renewable distributed 
generation into electricity networks and smart grids and on the application of soft computing 
methodologies to analysis and planning of power systems. In these fields, he has published more than 
140 technical papers including more than 60 international journal papers that received more than 900 
citations with an H-index equal to 18 according to Scopus. Dr. Siano is Associate Editor of the IEEE 
TRANSACTIONS ON INDUSTRIAL INFORMATICS, member of the editorial board of more than 25 
International Journals. He has been member of the Scientific Committee of many international 
conferences. He served as a reviewer and session chairman for many international conferences. He 
has been a Special Sessions Co-Chair of the IEEE-ISIE 2010. He was Guest Editor of many Special 
Sections of the IEEE TRANSACTIONS ON INDUSTRIAL ELECTRONICS. He is Vice-Chair of the 
Technical Committee on Smart Grids and a member of the Technical Committee on Renewable 
Energy Systems of the IEEE IES. He has been Aggregate Professor at University of Salerno of the 
following courses: • Electrical Systems I (2005) • Technology and economics of energy (2005-2014) • 
Electrical Systems II (2006) • Automation for Power Systems and Power Plants (2007-2009) • Energy 
Management (2011-2014) • Laboratory of Technology and economics of energy (2011-2012).
