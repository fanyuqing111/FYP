About Our Laboratory.... 
      Our research group had been focusing on the research of GaN-based optoelectronic materials and light emitting devices including LEDs, 
resonant cavity LEDs, Lasers and VCSELs for the past several years funded by the National Science Council and Ministry of Education under 
the Academic Center of Excellency program.
